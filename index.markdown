# UCA Construction Standards

Welcome to the University of Central Arkansas Construction Standards documentation. This site provides comprehensive building requirements and standards for all UCA construction projects.

## Quick Navigation

### [Instructions for Architects & Engineers](/standards/instructions/)
Meeting minutes, lactation rooms, uni-sex restrooms, and general instructions.

### [Procurement & Contracting](/standards/procurement/)
Weather delays, RFI procedures, electronic file release, and trench safety.

### [General Requirements](/standards/general-requirements/)
Substitution procedures, submittal procedures, temporary facilities, and project closeout.

### [Facility Construction](/standards/facility-construction/)
Standards for concrete, masonry, wood, thermal protection, openings, finishes, specialties, equipment, and furnishings.

### [Facility Services](/standards/facility-services/)
Fire suppression, plumbing, HVAC, electrical, communications, and electronic safety systems.

### [Site Work](/standards/site-work/)
Earthwork, exterior improvements, paving, landscaping, and site furnishings.

### [Sustainable Compliance](/standards/sustainable/)
Arkansas Sustainable Energy-Efficient Buildings Program requirements.
