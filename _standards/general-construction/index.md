\## Submittal Procedures

\### (Section 013300)

\- Contractor shall \*\*copy the UCA Construction Manager\*\* on all RFIs and Submittals.  

\- Architect shall \*\*forward a copy of all approved submittals\*\* to the Construction Manager \*\*immediately after approval\*\*.  

\- Architect shall \*\*copy the UCA Construction Manager\*\* on all \*\*RFI responses\*\* and \*\*Proposal Requests\*\* when issued. :contentReference\[oaicite:1]{index=1}

\## Temporary Facilities and Controls

\### (Section 015000)

\*\*Field Office\*\*  

Provide a conditioned field office large enough to accommodate an \*\*8' table and chairs\*\* and a \*\*plans table\*\*. The office will be made available to the architect and owner for site meetings as necessary.



\*\*Site Security \& Storage\*\*  

\- Install \*\*chain link fencing\*\* around the construction area to secure the building and materials storage.  

\- Provide \*\*separate storage facilities\*\* for tools and equipment. :contentReference\[oaicite:2]{index=2}

\## Construction Waste Management and Disposal

\### (Section 017419)

\- \*\*No debris\*\* of any kind may be disposed of on the Owner’s property.  

\- \*\*Waste disposal is the Contractor’s responsibility.\*\* :contentReference\[oaicite:3]{index=3}

\## Project Closeout

\### (Section 017700)

\*\*Project Record Documents (Digital As-Builts)\*\*  

\- Deliver a \*\*complete set of as-built drawings in digital format\*\* to the Physical Plant \*\*at Substantial Completion\*\*, along with paper copies.  

\- Provide \*\*digital as-built drawings\*\* for \*\*elevators\*\*, \*\*energy management systems\*\*, and \*\*similar controls\*\* at Substantial Completion. :contentReference\[oaicite:4]{index=4}



\*\*Operating \& Maintenance Data\*\*  

\- \*\*O\&M Manuals\*\* shall be delivered to the Owner \*\*before the Substantial Completion Date\*\*.  

\- The Architect shall \*\*prepare a checklist\*\* of all O\&M manuals during design and deliver it to the Construction Manager for the Owner’s approval.  

\- Provide \*\*two hard copies\*\* of each manual \*\*plus one electronic copy\*\*. :contentReference\[oaicite:5]{index=5}



\*\*Coordination / Formats\*\*  

\- \*\*Contact the UCA Physical Plant\*\* for preferred \*\*media\*\* and \*\*CAD software version\*\* prior to delivering digital files. :contentReference\[oaicite:6]{index=6}



\*\*Attic Stock\*\*  

Provide at Substantial Completion:  

\- \*\*Flooring:\*\* one full box and any open boxes of floor covering (carpet tiles, LVT, etc.).  

\- \*\*Paint:\*\* 1 gallon of each paint color.  

\- \*\*Filters/Belts:\*\* one complete change-out of HVAC filters; deliver a complete set of \*\*filters and belts\*\* to the Physical Plant.  

\- Physical Plant will advise which items remain in the building vs. which are delivered to the Plant. :contentReference\[oaicite:7]{index=7}
