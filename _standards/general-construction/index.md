## Substitution Procedures

### (Section 1)

1. UCA will have final approval of all substitutions.

## Submittal Procedures

### (Section 2)

1. Contractor to copy UCA construction manager with all RFIs and Submittals
2. Architect to forward a copy of all submittals to the construction manager immediately after approval.
3. Architect to copy UCA construction manager with all RFI responses and Proposal Requests when issued.

## Temporary Facilities and Controls

### (Section 3)

1. Field Office - Provide field office of sufficient size to accommodate an 8' table and chairs and large enough to hold a plans table, with conditioned air. The field office will be made available to the architect and owner for conducting site meetings as necessary.
2. Install chain link fence completely around construction area to secure building and materials storage area.
3. Provide separate storage facilities for tools, equipment, etc.

## Construction Waste Management and Disposal

### (Section 4)

1. Earthwork - **No** debris of any kind is to be disposed of on Owner's property.
2. Waste Disposal is the responsibility of the Contractor

## Project Closeout

### (Section 5)

Submit the following to the University Physical Plant prior to Substantial completion:

1. **Digital** copies of construction drawings, as-built drawings, elevator, energy management system, and similar control drawings
2. **Paper** copies of the as-built drawings.
3. Two physical copies and one digital copy of all Operating and Maintenance Manuals. Architect to confirm all required manuals with UCA Construction Manager during design.
4. **Attic Stock Requirements:**
   1. One full box and any open boxes of floor covering, carpet tiles, LVT, etc.;
   2. One gallon of each paint
   3. One complete change out of HVAC Filters.
   4. Physical Plant will discuss with the various crafts what to leave in the building and what to deliver to the physical plant.
   5. Deliver a complete set of filters and belts to the Physical Plant at substantial completion.
