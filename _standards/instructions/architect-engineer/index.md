## Submit plans to the Conway Fire Department

### (Section 1)

1. The state fire marshal does have review authority; however, he relies on the local jurisdiction for review.

## Conway Corporation Coordination

### (Section 2)

1. Complete Conway Corporation Utility Service Requirement [Form](https://media.conwayarkansas.gov/conwayarkansas-media/documents/Utility_Service_Request_Form_-_Engineering.pdf).
2. Meet with Conway Corp as needed to coordinate project utility requirements, and future CC plans.

## Meeting Minutes

### (Section 3)

1. The Design Team Lead shall be responsible for preparing and submitting minutes for all meetings between their office and University personnel (Including phone conversations for all phases of services covered under the Agreement Between Owner and Design Team.)
2. Submit Notes to the Owner's Representative no later than the third working day following the meeting/conversation. The Owner's Representative will distribute copies to the appropriate parties

## Room Numbering

### (Section 4)

1. Design Team will provide to the Owner a floor plan devoid of room numbers so the Owner may number the rooms.
   1. If the Design Team is directed by UCA to number the rooms:
      1. Use **four** (4) digit numbers for stair towers, corridors, and elevator lobbies.
      2. Number the rooms sequentially starting in the northwest corner and move clockwise around the building.

## Submit Digital Drawings to Physical Plant

### (Section 5)

1. A complete set of construction drawings in digital format shall be delivered to the Physical Plant at the same time paper copies of the bid set of construction drawings are delivered.

## Site Design

### (Section 6)

1. Ensure positive site drainage to prevent water from flooding buildings. Implement proper grading and drainage systems to direct water away from structures.
2. For buildings set away from paving with exterior MEP access, provide a 6-foot wide walkway to mechanical and electrical rooms. Design walkways with soft radii to accommodate UCA's golf cart "buggies."

## Roof Areas

### (Section 7)

1. All roof areas (in square feet) will be clearly stated on the plans.
2. For 'Steep Roofs' (over 2:12 pitch), measure along the slope to record true roofing areas.

## Floor drains

### (Section 8)

1. Ensure that any floors with drains are properly sloped toward the drain(s). This does not mean that wet areas must be depressed & filled-in, but simply that floor drains are clearly below the finished floors, so that the floor **WILL** drain.

## Air & Moisture Intrusion

### (Section 9)

1. Architects must design the building envelope to prevent infiltration of humid air that may condense on cold surfaces within the building, such as HVAC ducts and equipment. The design documents should specify appropriate moisture control systems and construction quality control inspection methods to ensure proper installation and performance of the building envelope in mitigating moisture intrusion and potential condensation issues.

## Floor Penetrations

### (Section 10)

1. Without exception, **all** floor penetrations shall be sealed to prevent any moisture or smoke migration.
