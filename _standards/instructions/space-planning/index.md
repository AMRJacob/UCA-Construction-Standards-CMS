## Lactation Rooms

### (Section 1)

1. In larger buildings *(not including dormitories and Greek houses)* include a Lactation Room including:
   1. Sink/counter
   2. Changing station
   3. Comfortable chair and side table
   4. Electrical outlets adjacent to chair
   5. **No** refrigerator

## Single Occupancy Restroom

### (Section 2)

1. In larger buildings, provide a convenient single occupancy restroom including:
   1. Fold-up changing table
   2. Additional Floor & counter space if possible
