## Hollow Metal Doors and Frames

### (Section 1)

1. Hollow metal jambs exterior 14 gauge, interior 16 gauge.

2. Welded joints (no knock-down frames).

3. Galvanized Exterior door frames??

## Wood Doors

### (Section 2)

1. Solid core 3' x 7', veneer / color to be determined

2. Provide small vision windows in **ALL** the private office doors and conference rooms – faculty & student areas.

3. If budget allows: Instead of vision window Provide a flush door with a glass sidelight the height of the door(s)

## Aluminum Framed Entrances (Doors)

### (Section 3)

1. Use approximate 6" wide-stile doors, where a door lite is included.

2. Use Min. 10" base stile for ADA compliance

3. Use Continuous Hinges.

4. If available, use standard replica divided lites, using interior \& exterior grilles with internal grille in the insulated glass space.

## Metal-Clad Wood Windows

### (Section 4)

1. Single hung or fixed, colonial lite, with thermal break

2. Aluminum Clad Wood

&nbsp;  1. Windows shall **NOT** be operable. (UCA Instruction) – NOTE: Operable windows are how TME was able to eliminate the outside air units and ductwork on the dorm renovations. Should operable not be an option for dorms and apartments?

3. Standard replica divided lites, using interior \& exterior grilles with internal grille in the insulated glass space.

4. Preferred Manufacturer: Pella

## Door Hardware

### (Section 5)

1. Finish Hardware color: 626 Finish

2. Door Lock Types:

&nbsp;  1. Classrooms and offices 93K9AB14D626

&nbsp;  2. Mechanical Areas 93K9D14D626

3. Keying:

&nbsp;  1. Best 7 pin locksets.

&nbsp;  2. UCA will do the coring, and will work with Best on the keying hierarchy & schedule. The cost of blank cores and keying will be in the contractor's contract.

4. Panic Devices: Von Duprin

5. P. 2 - Mechanical & Electrical room doors:

&nbsp;  1. Double doors - on the inactive leaf, interior use surface bolts with extenders as required

&nbsp;  2. Provide closer on active leaf.

6. Closers: LCN 4040XP

7. Hinges.

&nbsp;  1. Quality standard : Hager Hinge Company

&nbsp;  2. Other accepted: Stanley, Ives & McKinney

&nbsp;  3. Continuous gear hinges. National Guard, Stanley Select, all Heavy Duty

&nbsp;  4. Kick and Armor plates. 16 ga. Stainless steel

&nbsp;  5. P. 2 - Trimco is acceptable manufacturer for armor & kick plates.

## Automatic Door Operators

### (Section 6)

1. Power Assisted Openers: Stanley Magic Force, one or both leaves

## Glazing

### (Section 7)

**Exterior Glazing:**

1. The architect shall evaluate exterior glazing in conjunction with the mechanical engineer to determine the optimum types of glass to minimize energy consumption.

2. Factors to be considered along with economics shall include glazing thermal insulating factor as well as reflectivity, emissivity and transmittance.

3. **A brief report** of the glazing types considered and their effect on the life cycle energy cost considered shall be delivered to the University prior to final selection of exterior glazing materials.
