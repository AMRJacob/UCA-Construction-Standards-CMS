## Visual Display Boards

### (Section 1)

1. Consider electronic building directories – simpler \& potentially lower cost. Verify control point \& responsibility for keeping current

## Marker Boards

### (Section 2)

1. Provide simple chalk trays rather than recessed compartments.

## Building Plaques

### (Section 3)

1. Verify with Physical Plant if a Dedicatory Plaque for building is desired.

2. Interior wall mounted (onto drywall), Cast aluminum, wording by UCA, 20" x 36"

## Signage

### (Section 4)

1. **Exterior Monument signs** – **none** required. UCA will provide standard yard sign out of the FFE budget. OFOI. NOT lighted

2. **NO** directional signage at parking lots.

3. **Room Signage** – Allowance in contractor's contract - $100 per door.

&nbsp;  1. Archway Graphics is the preferred vendor. UCA will work with them on the sizes, color, naming, etc.

&nbsp;  2. Bart Lusk is the contact. 501-224-0227.

4. **Naming letters in the façade:**

&nbsp;  1. Verify location and actual text with Physical Plant.

&nbsp;  2. Cast Aluminum, Dark Bronze finish, Times New Roman font, Approximately 6" letter height, by Archway Graphics.

&nbsp;  3. Mounting is by concealed pins

&nbsp;  4. CFCI

5. **Stair Directional Signs** – IN the stairs, as required by code, in addition to Room identification sign outside the stair.

&nbsp;  1. One each floor landing, each required stair, as required by ICC.

## Corner Guards

### (Section 8)

1. IPC, Construction Specialties is specified, or approved equal.

&nbsp;  1. Comer Guard: AM-20 Locate from top of base to 4' long.

2. Materials: Vinyl/Acrylic:

&nbsp;  1. High impact extruded material with pebblette grain texture; as selected by Owner

&nbsp;  2. Aluminum retainers. 3063-T6 alloy: ASTM B 221.

3. Fasteners: Noncorrosive, compatible with aluminum retainers.

4. Surface mounted assembly consisting of continuous extruded aluminum retainer with snap on Acrovyn cover. Provide color match end caps and hardware for installation including mounting hardware.

## Fire Extinguishers, Cabinets, and Accessories

### (Section 9)

1. Prefer flush, recess mount.

2. Use bracket mounted (no enclosure) in engineering and other non-occupied or partly finished spaces.

## Lockers

### (Section 10)

1. Prefer built-in combination-type locks, with Owner's override key

## Flagpoles

### (Section 11)
