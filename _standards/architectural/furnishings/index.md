## Entrance Floor Mats and Frames

### (Section 1)

1. For high traffic locations, consider recessed \& drained roll-up aluminum removable mat with fiber insert strips.

2. If removable mat serves a building with heavy load – building without a delivery door, heavy supply loads – specify types with 'feet' designed to sustain such loads.

3. Make sure the walk-off mat is long enough in the direction of traffic to effectively remove soil \& water/snow.
