## Curbs and Sidewalks

### (Section 3)

1. Concrete Walks shall be a minimum 6' wide and 5" thick, psi 3500 lbs., reinforcement fiber mesh at the rate of 1.5 lbs. per cubic yard of concrete.

2. **Is air entrainment standard?**

3. **Is broom finish typical? Or other?**

4. **Is there a preferred cement & aggregate type/color?**

5. Typically locate new street-side sidewalks 60" clear from the back-of-curb, for pedestrian safety.

6. *Insert about raised detectable warnings.*

## Landscape Irrigation System

### (Section 5)

1. Rainbird system is preferred vendor.

2. Avoid drip irrigation and bubblers wherever possible – UCA prefers fewer larger/heads.

3. Include a RPZ with controller.

4. UCA does not like 'fake rocks' RPZ covers – locate in building if possible.

## Planting

### (Section 6)

1. UCA strongly prefers to minimize complicated, maintenance intensive plantings.

## Landscape Edging

### (Section 7)

\*\*Concrete landscape edging:\*\*

1. UCA standard edging contractor is The Custom Edge Concrete Contractor, Conway, AR, or approved equal. Contact Heath McWilliams, (501)730-8746.

2. Edging shall have no dyes added. Seal concrete after completion

3. Profile – Match the curvilinear concrete edging around the foundation plantings, north side of Wingo Hall.

&nbsp;  1. That profile is a very eased edge front \& back edge, with a ½" to 1" slope down from back to front.

&nbsp;  2. Provide control joints – very narrow, to match Wingo, every 30-36".
