\## AIA Document A201 – General Conditions for the Contract of Construction

\### (Section 004000A)

\*Reference: AIA A201 §4.3.7.2\*



\*\*Weather Delays (Allowance for Normal Inclement Weather)\*\*  

Include in the Contract Time an allowance for calendar days per month which, per the following table, may not be available for outdoor construction work.



| Month     | Days | Month     | Days |

|-----------|------|-----------|------|

| January   | 7    | July      | 6    |

| February  | 5    | August    | 4    |

| March     | 7    | September | 6    |

| April     | 7    | October   | 3    |

| May       | 8    | November  | 6    |

| June      | 6    | December  | 4    |



\*\*Time Extensions\*\*  

\- Contract Time will \*\*not\*\* be extended for \*normal\* inclement weather.  

\- Extensions will be considered only if the Contractor substantiates \*\*greater-than-normal\*\* inclement weather \*\*for the full term of Contract Time\*\* using a 5-year average of NOAA climatological data for the project locale \*\*and\*\* demonstrates actual delay to the Work.  

\- \*\*Submittal deadline:\*\* All inclement-weather day extension requests must be submitted to the Architect \*\*by the 15th of the following month\*\*.



\*\*Measure of Extreme Weather\*\*  

\- The measure is the \*\*number of days exceeding\*\* the monthly values above in which \*\*precipitation exceeded 0.10 inch\*\*, based on the same weather station/data source.  

\- If the \*\*total accumulated\*\* days lost to weather \*\*exceed\*\* the accumulated expected total from the table, the time for completion will be extended by the \*\*number of calendar days\*\* needed to include the excess.

\## Architects’ Electronic File Release

\### (Section 006520)

\- \*\*AIA-specific forms are not required\*\* for this purpose.  

\- \*\*Document ownership:\*\* UCA is considered to “own” the documents and, as a matter of policy, will \*\*not pay the Architect\*\* for electronic drawing files.  

\- UCA \*\*may sign a release of liability\*\* to obtain the electronic files.  

\- \*\*Contractor payment:\*\* Consult the UCA Project Manager regarding whether the \*\*Contractor may pay\*\* for the electronic files.  

\- \*\*Sharing requirement:\*\* Subject to payment and liability release agreements, the Architect shall \*\*share electronic files with the Contractor\*\* for clash detection, shop drawing preparation, and related needs.  

\- \*\*File formats:\*\* Provide files in their \*\*native formats\*\* (2D/3D models), as \*\*PDFs\*\*, and/or \*\*translated\*\* formats \*\*within the Architect’s ability\*\*.
