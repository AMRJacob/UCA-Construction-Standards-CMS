# Instructions for Architects & Engineers

## Meeting Minutes

The Lead Consultant shall be responsible for taking notes at all meetings between their office and University personnel, including telephone conversations for all phases of services covered under the Contract Agreement.

- Submit to Owner's Representative no later than the **third** working day following the meeting or telephone conversation
- The Owner's Representative will distribute copies to the appropriate parties

---

## Required Spaces

### Lactation Rooms

In larger buildings (not including dormitories and Greek houses), typically include a Lactation Room.

**Requirements:**
- Provide with sink, counter and changing station
- Include space for a comfortable chair and a side table, with electrical outlets close by
- No refrigerator

### Uni-Sex Restrooms

In larger buildings, provide a convenient Uni-sex / 'Family' restroom.

**Requirements:**
- Include a fold-up changing table
- Provide more floor & counter space if possible

## Checklist

### Submit plans to the Conway Fire Department
The state fire marshal does have review authority; however, he relies on the local jurisdiction for review.

### Conway Corporation Coordination
Complete Conway Corporation Utility Service Requirement Form.

### Meet with Conway Corp as needed
Coordinate project utility requirements and future CC plans.

### Provide floor plan devoid of room numbers
Provide to the Owner a floor plan devoid of room numbers so the Owner may number the rooms.

**If Consultant is directed by UCA to number the rooms:**
- Use four (4) digit numbers for stair towers, corridors, and elevator lobbies
- Number the rooms sequentially starting in the northwest corner and move clockwise around the building

### Submit complete set of construction drawings
Submit a complete set of construction drawings in digital format to the Physical Plant at the same time paper copies of the bid set of construction drawings are delivered.

- Contact the UCA Physical Plant Department for preferred media and CAD software version
